Website Evaluations

Website 1: FromJapan

FromJapan is a proxy bidding website that allows western users to bid on and purchase goods from Japan.

  • Contrast - The dark text color contrasts the white background, and the shade of blue on the links is still readable on the white background too.
  • Repetition - The design for selecting categories of items and then items for sale/auction themselves is consistent within themselves, so are tabs and headings.
  • Alignment - Alike items are center aligned into a grid to keep their styling consistent, and so they can all be recognized for their function easily.
  • Proximity - Alike item categories, and from there purchase/biddable items are also styled in the same grid as one another to link their similarities.

Website 2: A Super Smash Brothers Melee fan site that dates back to November 2002

This website appears to be a website dedicated to chronicling information about the 2001 platform fighting game Super Smash Brothers Melee. It carries information and images about both characters and items in the game. This is a page I've had bookmarked for a while for humorous purposes but the fact that I can actually use it for the bad website example on this assignment in this class makes it even better.

  • Contrast - Contrast on the main page is AWFUL, has blue hyperlinks placed on top of a blue glitter background image. Also, if you go to any character's intro page, the text is placed directly on top of whatever background image for the charcater is there.
  • Repetition - The layout between each type of page happens to be relatively consistent, however some character introduction pages tend to have alignment variance between each (left vs right vs centered)
  • Alignment - Alignment for tables is very standard, but again text alignment is inconsistent or misleading on certain pages such as Pokeball pulls
  • Proximity - Alike content on character moveset pages do in fact stick together (regular attacks vs special attacks have separate design, but each type stay closer to alikes than others)
