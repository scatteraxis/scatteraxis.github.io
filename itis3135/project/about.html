About Prince Hycy Bull Jr.

"I am a 24-year old freelance architect that graduated from the Georgia Institute of Technology, and am now currently studying for my Master's degree in Architecture. For the past five years, I have studied the intricacies of Architecture and Graphic Design to hone my craft, and eventually contribute to something greater.

Although most of my technical skills stem from my education, I hold dearly many lessons from my Sierra Leonian descent. In my culture, there is a strong sense of community, where it takes a village to raise a child. To truly learn, is to learn with others as you take steps to grow in tandem.

Architecture and Design is much more than just putting buildings and art into the site. It's a way to interconnect with the "village" you build with your team, and the communities you with to impact with a message of comraderie.

The type of designer I strive to be is one that disparages belief that the praxis of working in isolation proves fruitless, as you'll always need someone's guidance at every level."
