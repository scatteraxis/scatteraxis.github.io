Projects

This page consists of the numerous projects that Prince has worked on over his academic career. The images can be clicked to cycle through.

PANGLIN

The Photovoltaic Activated Network for the Generation of Life-Saving Initiatives (PANGLIN) is an architecture project focused on developing a structure to be used for the emergency control and relief for a town or city. It is also intended to be extremely eco-conscious by utilizing net zero environmental impact structures.

Terraine de Jeau

Terraine de Jeau is a project that aims to create a sustainable park that promotes eco-friendliness and serves as a model for sustainable urban development. The park will provide a space for people to connect with nature, engage in outdoor activities, and learn about sustainable living practices.

Faltenweglich

The aim of Faltenweglich was to design a mobile structure for a public space, which can serve a range of cultural events. It takes inspiration from the Origami concept of tessellation, which refers to an arrangement of polygonal shapes closely fitted together in a repeated pattern without gaps or overlapping.
